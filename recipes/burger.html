Burger

Delicious burger recipe

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Burger buns
  • American cheese
  • Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Special sauce

Steps

  1. Toast buns
  2. Combine mayo, ketchup and pickles into special sauce
  3. Form patties and cook in preheated pan
  4. Melt cheese
  5. Assemble burger bun
  6. Enjoy!
